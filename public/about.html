About DebateLive

Learn about our mission, our team, and why we're passionate about fostering meaningful debates.

Our Story

DebateLive was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to create a platform where important ideas could be discussed, challenged, and refined through structured debate.

In a world of echo chambers and polarized discourse, we believe that respectful debate between knowledgeable participants is essential for progress and understanding.

What started as a small project has grown into a global community of critical thinkers, experts, and curious minds who come together to explore complex topics through the time-tested format of formal debate.

Our Values

Intellectual Integrity

We believe in honest, fact-based discourse that prioritizes truth over winning arguments.

Respectful Dialogue

Even when discussing contentious topics, we maintain an environment of mutual respect and civility.

Intellectual Diversity

We actively seek out diverse perspectives and believe the best ideas emerge from the clash of different viewpoints.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I participate in a debate?

We select debaters based on expertise and experience. If you're interested in participating, please fill out our application form and our team will review your credentials.

Can I suggest topics for future debates?

We welcome topic suggestions from our community. You can submit your ideas through our contact form or email us directly.

How are debates moderated?

All debates are overseen by experienced moderators who ensure that rules are followed, time limits are respected, and the discussion remains civil and productive.

Is DebateLive available internationally?

Yes! DebateLive is a global platform. We host debates with participants from around the world and our content is accessible internationally.
